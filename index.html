History

Founded in 1920, East Bengal Club is based in Kolkata, India. It has a rich history in football and has been one of the strongest teams in Indian football, particularly in the I-League and other tournaments.

Teams

East Bengal Club has several teams, including its main football team that competes in top leagues and tournaments. The club has a passionate fan base and is known for its rivalries with other top teams like Mohun Bagan.

Upcoming Events

The club regularly hosts football matches and events, including home games, training camps, and charity events. Stay tuned for upcoming matches!
